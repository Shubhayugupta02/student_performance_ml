About CyberML

B.Tech Project

CyberML is a student performance analytics portal built with Flask, SQLite and scikit-learn.

Teachers can upload student result datasets, train machine learning models, create assignments and grade submissions. The system computes grade distributions, feature importances and risk alerts.

Students can experiment with a prediction form that uses attendance, study hours, assignment completion and internals to estimate expected final marks and grade, with personalised tips.

The cyber-neon UI is designed to feel like a futuristic control panel while remaining simple enough for classroom use.
